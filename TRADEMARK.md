# Trademark and branding policy

The `wakeword-forge` name, logo, and project identity identify the original project.

## Allowed uses

You may use the name to:

- Refer factually to the project.
- State that your work uses, forks, or is compatible with wakeword-forge.
- Preserve copyright, license, NOTICE, citation, and attribution information.

## Not allowed

You may not use the `wakeword-forge` name or branding in a way that suggests:

- Your fork, package, model, hosted service, or integration is the official project.
- The maintainer endorses your project or service.
- Your generated models or datasets are official wakeword-forge releases.

Forks and integrations should use distinct names unless written permission is granted.
