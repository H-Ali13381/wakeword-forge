# Commercial use and services

wakeword-forge code is licensed under Apache-2.0. Commercial use is allowed when the license and NOTICE requirements are followed.

Organizations can use the open-source package without asking for separate permission, but they remain responsible for:

- Preserving required license and NOTICE information.
- Verifying third-party package, model, dataset, voice, and generated-output licenses.
- Ensuring any user recordings or published models have proper consent and provenance.

## Optional paid help

Paid help may be available for:

- Custom wake-word evaluation.
- Integration into local agent or voice-assistant stacks.
- ONNX/runtime packaging.
- Dataset/provenance review.
- Deployment support and latency tuning.

The open-source project remains usable without a paid agreement.
